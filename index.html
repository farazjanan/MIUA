Welcome to MIUA 2026

The 28th UK Conference on Medical Image Understanding and Analysis (MIUA) will be held at Imperial College London, located in the cultural heart of South Kensington. Join us for three days of insightful discussions, technical presentations, and networking opportunities.

Scope

MIUA is a multidisciplinary forum for researchers, clinicians, and industry leaders working in medical imaging and computational analysis. Key areas include:

  • Image Acquisition and Reconstruction
  • Deep Learning and Machine Learning for Imaging
  • Quantitative Imaging Biomarkers
  • Novel Imaging Techniques
  • Multimodal Imaging
